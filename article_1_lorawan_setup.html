A LoRaWAN setup tale of spring 2024

Late of autumn 2024, year of the dragon, company off-site. While scratching our heads to get an idea to implement during a hackathon, a colleague of mine introduced me to the world of LoRa and LoRaWAN.
For those new to IoT (like me), here is TL;DR to save you some clicks:

LoRa is the radio signal that carries the data - i.e. a physical layer.
LoRaWAN is the communication protocol that defines how that data is sent across the network - i.e. a transport layer.
We got so excited that my jet lag vanished. We ordered the equipment and waited for it to be delivered. And we waited... We waited... until no time remained, so we dropped the idea and continued working on something else. The so-anticipated harware arrived the last day of the hackathon, which was obviously too late. We decided to get the equipment back to our home-offices and start working on it when our capacity allowed it.
