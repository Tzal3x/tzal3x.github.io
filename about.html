About me

This is a timeline of my education and career as a software engineer.

Starting from my B.Sc. in Statistics, at first everything revolved around data science and math, but it wasn't until my M.Sc. in Computer Science, and my first role in a startup where CS was where I found what really suited me.

From there on, I've been working as a full stack software engineer, with a focus on backend development. Currently, I am working at Mysten Labs, a startup that created the Sui blockchain, and aims to onboard the next generation of web3 applications.
